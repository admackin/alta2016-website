Attending ALTA

Registration

Workshop registration ($80 for salaried workers and free for students) is now open at Trybooking. All attendees must register before the event.

Venue

The conference is located at the Caulfield Campus of Monash University.

Accomodation

There are few accomodation options close to the Caulfield Campus, but there are frequent train links from the Melbourne CBD. Attendees are encouraged to investigate accomodation options close to Flinders St station and travel in by train, which takes about 20 minutes. You will need to take either the Pakenham/Cranbourne train or the Frankston/Stony Point train and alight at the Caulfield stop (see train map for details; and the station on the map shown above.) To travel on a train you will need a Myki card, which lets you through the barriers at either end of your trip. This can be picked up from the station, using the ticket vending machines or from a service counter for $7 and come with a miserly amount of credit sufficient for a single trip. A trip lasting up to two hours (which can use a mix of train, tram and bus) costs $3.90 for full-fare travel. See Myki Money on the PTV website for details. Note that PTV are not very friendly about concession fares for interstate visitors, if you think you might be eligble please do check carefully as the ticket inspectors are rarely friendly.

You may find booking.com useful for booking the accomoodation. Some good options can be "Mercure Welcome Melbourne" and "Aura on Flinders Serviced Apartments".
