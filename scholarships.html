Due to the generous support of our sponsors, ALTA will be offering travel support for students to attend and present at ALTA 2015 in Parramatta.

Types of Scholarships

This year, we will be offering two types of scholarships and you should be clear in your application which scholarship you would like to be considered for.

Travel + Accommodation: Funding to cover the costs of airfares to Sydney airport plus 3 nights' accommodation at UWS Village (Parramatta campus)

Accommodation Only: 3 nights' accommodation at UWS Village (Parramatta campus)

Unsuccessful applicants for the Travel + Accommodation scholarship will automatically be considered for the Accommodation Only scholarship.

How to Apply

Students wishing to be considered for travel support must apply by email to the organisers (workshop@alta.asn.au) by 20 November. Preference will be given to students who will be presenting a paper at the workshop. However, we encourage all students who would like to attend to apply as we have the support to be generous with our awards.

Applications should include:

  • Your full name
  • The name of your research supervisor
  • Where you will be travelling from
  • Which scholarship you would like to be considered for
  • A brief outline addressing the selection criteria below (bullet points is fine)

Please also arrange for your supervisor to send a separate email acknowledging that you are indeed enrolled as a student, and that the statements you made in the application are correct.

Selection Criteria

Applications will be selected according to the following criteria:

  • Will you be presenting a paper at ALTA 2015?
  • What stage of your PhD candidature are you at?
  • Have you received ALTA Student Travel Support previously?
  • Is your university and/or supervisor unable to fund (or fully fund) your travel?

Small Print

The organising committee reserves the right to make awards as they see fit, or to alter the amount of any awards.

Note that the award will be in the form of a reimbursement of expenses, not a cash award. The awardees will only receive money, up to the value of the award, after producing receipts for appropriate expenses, and completing any supplied forms.
